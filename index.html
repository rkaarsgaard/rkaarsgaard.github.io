Robin Kaarsgaard

I am (tenure-track) assistant professor in the Department of Mathematics and Computer Science at University of Southern Denmark. There, I am associated with ACP and the Centre for Quantum Mathematics.

My research focuses on quantum foundations and quantum programming languages (especially their semantics), and more broadly on the applications of category theory in computer science.
